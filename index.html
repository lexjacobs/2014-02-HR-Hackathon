2014 Open Data Challenge

Rental GPS coordinate visualization for all bike rentals in January, 2014.

As the days advance through January, 2014, the start / end positions of each bike rental are shown as the movement of a single dot. In aggregate, the major rental patterns become apparent. Notably, Market Street, 2nd Street, and the Caltrain station.

Rendering the start / end coordinates of 333 bike trips on day 1 of January, 2014

Created during a short hackathon at: by Alex Jacobs. Open-source code available on GitHub. Rendering engine: d3js.org
